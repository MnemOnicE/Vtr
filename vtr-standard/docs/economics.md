# Economic Model: Net Metering & DePIN

**Status:** Draft

## Overview

The VTR protocol enables a decentralized physical infrastructure network (DePIN) for verified human data. This model incentivizes users to contribute high-quality, authentic video data for AI training and other uses.

## The Asset: Certified Organic Data

Verified human reality is a scarce resource. .vtr files provide a guarantee of origin, making them valuable for training AI models that suffer from "Model Collapse" when trained on synthetic data.

## Settlement Mechanism: "Invisible Wallet"

1.  **Minting:** When a user records video with a valid VTR signature, $VTR tokens are minted.
2.  **Net Metering:** Users do not need to manage a crypto wallet. A "Data Trust" manages the conversion of $VTR tokens.
3.  **Credit:** The value is applied as fiat credits directly to the user's mobile carrier bill.

## Buyers

*   **AI Companies:** Purchase access to the "Human Data Pool" for model training.
*   **Municipalities/News:** Verify citizen journalism and evidence.
